YOUR PROFILE

You can create your own private space in the world of Sitorbis.

You can create your personal profile in just a few steps so that we can get to know you better and provide you with the most accurate information about your particular interests. Moreover, you will be able to complete and customize your profile information at any time.

Start Your Digital Route

The modul gives an empty map of the World for Sitorbis’s each member. In this way, The Route Modul is going to create a map of the special interest tourism universe.

Pin Your Locations

They can also pin their locations step by step to clearly explain their experiences to the followers.

Add Your Experiences

The members will be able to start a new route of a particular type of special interest tourism at any time and these routes will only include their personal experiences and adventures.

Share Contents & Get Comments

The member also can share descriptions and photos about their locations and their followers can make comments to what they share.

Instant Conversation System

During creating the route, they will be online. In this way their followers can follow them step by step and start an instant conversation to learn more about their common interests.

Pause & Create Later

The members can give a break to the route and keep going to create later. They can also reach their routes whenever they want and improve them with adding new information.

Be Match, Get Connect and
Travel Together

Sitorbis will enable you to match other people with similar travel habits, chat, and make new travel friends based on your interests and behavior on our website. For this, your travel experiences shared on your profile, your digital route, written and visual content that you like and comment on will be utilized.
